Our Story

We're making sustainable fashion accessible for growing families while building a community of conscious consumers.

Our Mission

Founded in 2023, KidsSwap was born from a simple observation: kids grow fast, but their clothes still have lots of life left in them. We created a platform where parents can easily buy and sell pre-loved children's clothing, reducing waste and making quality clothing more affordable.

  • Promoting sustainable fashion choices
  • Building a community of conscious parents
  • Making quality clothing accessible to all
