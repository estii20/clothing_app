Returns & Refunds

We aim to build trust in our community. Here's how we handle returns.

🔄 Return Policy

All items sold on KidsSwap are final sale. However, if an item arrives significantly different from its description (wrong size, visible damage not disclosed, etc.), you may request a review for a refund.

📝 How to Request a Return

If you receive an incorrect or misrepresented item, please contact us within 3 days of delivery through your account with clear photos and a description of the issue.

🔒 Buyer Protection

We protect buyers by holding funds until the seller confirms shipment and tracking. Disputes are handled fairly by our support team.

⚠️ What Can't Be Returned

  • Items damaged after delivery
  • Incorrect sizing due to buyer error
  • Normal wear consistent with "used" condition
