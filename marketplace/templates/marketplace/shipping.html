Shipping Information

Learn how shipping works when you buy or sell items on KidsSwap.

📦 Processing Time

Items are typically processed and shipped within 2–3 business days after purchase. Sellers are responsible for packaging and shipping their sold items.

🚚 Shipping Methods

We use standard shipping via USPS or UPS. Tracking information is provided once the item is shipped. Shipping rates are calculated based on weight and distance.

🌍 Shipping Areas

Currently, we only support shipping within the United States. We’re working to expand to more regions soon!

🔄 Returns & Exchanges

All sales are final. As a peer-to-peer platform, we encourage buyers to review listings and ask questions before purchasing. If an item is not as described, you can report the issue for resolution.
